Slovenian Synonyms Dictionary for Kindle readers

Compiled using the following datasets:

  • Thesaurus of Modern Slovene 1.0
  • Collocations Dictionary of Modern Slovene KSSS 1.0
  • Morphological lexicon Sloleks 2.0 - Clarin-SI
  • https://www.clarin.si/repository/xmlui/handle/11356/1364

All datasets are released under Creative Commons licenses by researchers of the Centre for Language Resources and Technologies, University of Ljubljana.
